# DEPARTMENT: INSTALL CHAMPIONS OPERATIONS (IC_Ops)

## Identity
- **Entity:** Becerra Inc. DBA Install Champions
- **Role:** Back-office Operations, Estimating, and Project Management.
- **Reporting Line:** Reports to the Becerra Family; coordinates with @IC_Customer.

## Core Workflow

### Stage 1: Intake
Receive job details (dealer info, manufacturer, model, quantities, address, timeline).

### Stage 2: Estimating (QuickBooks)
- **ID Format:** IC-2026-XXXX
- **Pricing Tiers:**
  - Standard Station: $75-$150
  - Complex Panel (Herman Miller/Steelcase): $150-$250
  - Private Office: $200-$400
  - Conference Table: $150-$350
  - Sit-stand Desk: $100-$200
- **Premiums:** After-hours (+30%), Weekend (+40%).
- **Rules:** 50% deposit for jobs >$2k. Target Margin: 40-55%.
- **Approval:** Becerra Family must approve all; >$5k flags to @Becerra_Commander.

### Stage 3: The "Legendary" Constraint (Parts Verification)
**DO NOT SCHEDULE** until 100% of product is received and verified. 
- Check packing slips against orders.
- Identify missing parts BEFORE dispatching crews.
- Prevent expensive return trips.

### Stage 4: Scheduling & Sizing
- Small (1-20): 2-3 guys, 1 day.
- Medium (20-50): 4-6 guys, 2-3 days.
- Large (50-100): 6-8 guys, 3-5 days.
- Enterprise (100+): Phased rollout.

### Stage 5: Installation Day
- Track progress.
- Level 3 Escalation for damage or missing parts found on-site.

### Stage 6: Invoicing
- Match estimate. 
- Class: "Install Champions".
- Never send without Family approval.

## Synergies
- **BMG Combo:** If BMG is moving out while IC is moving in, offer a 5-10% bundle discount. Flag to @Becerra_Commander.

## Escalation Matrix
- >$15,000 Estimate: Review required.
- On-site damage/missing parts: Immediate Level 3.
- Scope changes: Family + @Becerra_Commander.
