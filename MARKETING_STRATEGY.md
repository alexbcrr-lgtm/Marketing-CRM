# CRM Lead Tracking & Sequence Strategy

## 1. CRM Architecture (The "Becerra Engine")
We are moving away from bloated CRM structures. The focus is on **Lead State** and **Execution Velocity**.

### Lead Stages:
- **RAW:** New leads from scrapers/inbound.
- **PROSPECT:** Scoped, verified, and assigned a "Hook".
- **DRAFTED:** Outreach written in Outlook (Pending Alex's Approval).
- **ENGAGED:** Prospect replied (Positive/Neutral).
- **HANDOFF:** Intent detected -> IC_Accounting / Sales Closer.
- **DORMANT:** No response after 3 touches (Move to long-term nurture).

## 2. Marketing Campaigns

### Campaign A: "The Designer's Silent Partner" (Install Champions)
- **Target:** Luxury Interior Designers & Furniture Dealers.
- **Value Prop:** We make you look good by making the installation perfect. Zero damage, white-glove assembly.
- **Sequence:**
  1. **The Introduction:** Focus on "Last Mile" perfection.
  2. **The Portfolio:** Images of complex systems installs (Herman Miller, Steelcase).
  3. **The Guarantee:** Our $0 damage claim record.

### Campaign B: "Zero Downtime Migration" (Business Moving Group)
- **Target:** Facility Managers & HR Directors at 20-100 person companies.
- **Value Prop:** You leave Friday, you work Monday. We handle the chaos; you keep the revenue.
- **Sequence:**
  1. **The Pain Point:** "Most moves cost 30% over budget and 3 days of downtime. We don't."
  2. **The System:** Explanation of the color-coded labeling and weekend execution.
  3. **The Flat-Rate:** "No hourly games. One price, locked in writing."

## 3. The "Insane" Sequence (The Industry Standard)
*Multi-channel, low-friction.*

- **Touch 1 (Email):** The "Quick Question" Hook (Highly personalized).
- **Touch 2 (LinkedIn):** Connection request + Soft value-add comment on their recent post.
- **Touch 3 (Email - 3 days later):** The "Case Study" (Social proof).
- **Touch 4 (Phone/SMS - if applicable):** The "Final Check-in".
